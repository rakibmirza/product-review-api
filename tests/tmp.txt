Al-Driven Proactive Billing Dispute Prediction and Resolution Assistant
Summary of the Use Case:
Billing disputes

This use case proactively identifies potential billing disputes before they occur and suggest corrective
in telecom often arise due to incorrect charges usage anomalies, or delayed adjustments
actions. By analysing customer usage patterns, billing history, and complaint trends, the system predicts
high-risk scenarios and generates clear explanations or resolution steps for customer service teams This
reduces dispute volumes and improves customer trust.
Problem / Challenge Being Addressed:

Billing disputes are a major pain point for Telcos especially in B2B accounts where invoices are large and
complex. Disputes lead to delayed payments, strained relationships, and increased operational costs. For
example, a corporate customer may raise a dispute over unexpected roaming charges or incorrect
discounts applied to their monthly bill. Traditionally, disputes are handled reactively after the customer
complaints, requiring manual investigation and multiple escalations. GenAl can address this by predicting
anomalies in advance, flagging invoices likely to be challenged, and providing proactive communication or
adjustments, reducing friction and improving cash flow.
